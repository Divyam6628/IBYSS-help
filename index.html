About IBYSS

IBYSS is a registered non‑profit organisation dedicated to bringing hope and dignity to under‑served communities. Through an integrated approach that focuses on education, healthcare, nutrition and support for the elderly, we strive to break the cycle of poverty and create sustainable change. Our mission is simple: to save lives by harnessing the power of kindness.

Our Programs

Education

We sponsor tuition, supplies and teacher training to ensure children have access to a quality education.

Healthcare

Our medical camps, surgeries and hospital partnerships deliver life‑saving treatment to those who cannot afford it.

Elder Care

We provide companionship, nutritious meals and essential supplies to vulnerable seniors who would otherwise be alone.

Nutrition

Our feeding programmes ensure that no child goes to bed hungry by distributing nutritious meals every day.
