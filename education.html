About the Education Program

Our Education Program sponsors tuition, supplies and teacher training to ensure children have access to a quality education. By providing school materials, building infrastructure and supporting teachers, we strive to break the cycle of poverty through knowledge and learning.

  • Scholarships and tuition support for under‑served children
  • Books, uniforms and other essential school supplies
  • Teacher training and professional development
  • Classroom renovation and construction

With your generous support, we can continue to expand our reach and provide opportunities for children to thrive academically and beyond.
