About the Nutrition Program

Our Nutrition Program ensures that vulnerable children and families receive wholesome meals throughout the year. By providing balanced nutrition, we combat hunger and support healthy growth and development.

  • Daily meal distribution to children in need
  • Nutrition education for parents and caregivers
  • Food baskets for families facing food insecurity
  • Collaborations with local communities for sustainable farming

Your support keeps our kitchens running and plates full. Help us make hunger history for those who need our help most.
